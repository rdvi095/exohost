## What Is ExoHost?

ExoHost is a Nigerian web hosting company operating under OmoolaEx Ltd. They run on a Tier 3 Datacenter infrastructure with LiteSpeed-powered servers and NVMe SSD storage — the kind of setup you'd normally find with mid-tier international providers, but billed in Naira and supported locally.

The whole pitch is pretty straightforward: fast website, local support, Naira billing. For most Nigerian businesses and bloggers, that covers 90% of what you actually need.
